Apple Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976. 
The company is headquartered in Cupertino, California.
Tim Cook became CEO of Apple in 2011 after Steve Jobs resigned.
Apple acquired Beats Electronics in 2014 for approximately 3 billion dollars.
Microsoft and Apple have been long-time competitors in the technology industry.
Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft since 2014.
